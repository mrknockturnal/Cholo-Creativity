Boipelo Cholo

Founder & Head of Strategy

Boipelo Cholo is the founder of Cholo&Creativity, not only does her passion for research allow insight to unleash her creativity but her background in marketing strategy has advanced her career to build relationships with prospects, based on industry trends and knowledge. From Lesoba Difference as a strategist on brands such as Air Traffic & Navigation Services, Nedbank, Momentum, and Aon saw her developing, integrated dynamic Through-the-line marketing strategies that were seamless and solution driven.

With a unique combination of skills and talent at lkineo she thrived in CRM conceptual development and strategy implementation for Jeep, Fiat, Chrysler, Alfa Romeo and Dodge. With the knowledge of the ever evolving industry trends, she developed and recommended innovative CRM strategies to promote & address advertising needs of the accounts she worked on.

She has in-depth knowledhe of CRM, consumer insights, data, content management, SEO/SEM, Google analytics and web design. She is innovative, creative and obsessed with results. Boipelo graduated from AAA School of Advertising with a Bachelor's Degree in Marketing & Communications with a specialisation in Digital Marketing and is currently pursuing her post graduate business administration studies.
